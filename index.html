ChatPools

Dive into the community

ChatPools is an Open Source chat client focused on it's community.

The main goal of ChatPools is to allow to it's users to make "Pools" (Chat rooms)
based on their interest for communicating with people with the same interests.

Features

Simple and Intuitive search bar

Customize your profile

Be safer with a word filter

The sounds of the User Interface are random
